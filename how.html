How it Works

Streamline Your SaaS Offerings with Custom AI Solutions from Prompted LLC

1. Tailored Consultation and Model Fine-Tuning

Initial Discovery: We start with a discovery call to understand your SaaS platform's needs, focusing on enhancing AI capabilities specific to your domain and client requirements.
Model Fine-Tuning: Optimize your AI models for enhanced performance and customer experience, ensuring your software adapts to user needs and market dynamics.

2. Strategy Development and 'RAG' Architecture

Strategic AI Roadmap: Develop a strategic AI roadmap that aligns with your software's current capabilities and future growth aspirations.
'RAG' Architecture: Leverage our proprietary hybrid methods combining graph databases with vector space models for advanced data retrieval and processing.

3. Custom AI Development and Benchmarking AI Models

Prototyping and Testing: Quick turnaround with rapid prototyping to refine AI functionalities before deployment.
Benchmarking AI Models: Evaluate and benchmark your existing AI solutions against industry standards to identify opportunities for improvement.

4. Implementation, Optimization, and AI System Audits

Seamless Integration: Integrate custom AI smoothly with minimal disruption, following best practices for secure and efficient implementation.
AI System Audits: Conduct comprehensive audits of your AI systems focusing on cost efficiency and security to ensure compliance and savings.

5. Training, Support, and Strategic Communication

Empowerment Through Training: Equip your team with the knowledge to utilize and maintain the new AI systems to their fullest potential.
Strategic Communication: Infuse best practices in strategic communication and audience analysis to make your software the top performer for your specific client base.
