Who We Are
Ubiquity: The Core of AI’s Brave New World

Welcome to Ubiquity, the flagship platform by Prompted LLC that unites Builders, Extenders/Sellers, and Business Owners into the most innovative ecosystem AI has ever seen. This is where the courage of early adopters meets the power of community, and where the rules of value exchange are rewritten to usher in a new era of digital transformation.

The Power of Three: Builders, Extenders, and Business Owners

Builders: The architects and innovators who create the frameworks that push the boundaries of AI. They design solutions that are as agile as they are ambitious, laying the foundation for what’s possible in this rapidly evolving world.

Extenders/Sellers: The strategists and integrators who bring AI to life. They refine and scale groundbreaking ideas, ensuring that innovations reach the right markets and deliver maximum impact. Their expertise in adoption is the bridge between technology and profit.

Business Owners: The pragmatic visionaries who anchor innovation in real-world needs. They invest in and implement AI tools with a focus on tangible outcomes, providing essential feedback that drives continuous improvement.

Together, these cohorts don’t just coexist—they strengthen and uplift each other at every stage of AI adoption. Their shared excitement, engagement, and bravery have turned the earliest waves of AI integration into an ecosystem where innovation thrives, and where intangible currencies—trust, reputation, community influence—are finally valued as they should be.
