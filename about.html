About Breyden

A Journey from Ethical AI Advocacy to Tangible Solutions

In early 2023, Breyden Taylor embarked on a transformative journey, evolving from a blog on ethical AI use into Prompted LLC, a beacon for AI optimization and human experience enhancement.

As an Adjunct Professor at Purdue University with a rich background in human resources and operations management, Breyden's passion for optimization, employee engagement, and expert communication is the cornerstone of Prompted LLC.

Prompted LLC’s mission is to harmonize human potential with AI innovation. We stand firmly on values of transparency, authenticity, trust, and a people-centric approach. We partner with visionaries, guiding them from their current state to an AI-enhanced future.

We invite you to be part of this transformative integration of AI, crafted with integrity, humanity, and progress.

Tailoring AI Solutions for Nonprofits and Small Companies

Our approach to AI integration is grounded in flexibility and a deep understanding that AI needs vary significantly. At Prompted LLC, we specialize in integrating AI into your existing workflows, particularly focusing on the unique needs of nonprofits and small companies.
