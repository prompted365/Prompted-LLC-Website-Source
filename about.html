About Breyden

How We Got Started

In early 2023, Breyden Taylor, the visionary founder of Prompted LLC, embarked on a remarkable journey. What began as a humble blog exploring the ethical use of AI soon evolved into something greater - a quest to optimize AI, making it a catalyst for enhancing the essence of human experiences.

As an Adjunct Professor at Purdue University, Breyden brought his profound expertise to the classroom, honing his background in human resources and operations management. His passion lies in optimization, employee engagement, and effective communication, skills that formed the bedrock of Prompted LLC's mission.

With a mission to bridge the gap between human potential and AI innovation, Prompted LLC stands upon a strong foundation of core values. Transparency, authenticity, trust, and a people-centric approach define our commitment to every client. These values shape our journey as we work alongside visionaries, helping them navigate the path from their current state to their envisioned future.

Join us as we lead the way in the ethical and transformative integration of AI. At Prompted LLC, we're not just shaping the future; we're shaping it with integrity, humanity, and progress.
